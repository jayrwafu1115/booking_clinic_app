Who uses the platform

Patient / Public

Books appointments via the clinic's public page or embedded widget — no account required.

🏥

Clinic Owner

Manages the full clinic: doctors, services, schedules, billing & reports.

🩺

Doctor

Views own schedule & patient queue. Updates appointment status.

📋

Receptionist / Staff

Creates & manages appointments, walks in patients, handles daily operations.

🛡️

Super Admin

Platform-level oversight — manages all clinics, users & subscriptions.

Integrations & infrastructure
🗄️

Supabase (PostgreSQL)

Cloud database with row-level security. Every clinic's data is fully isolated.

💸

PayMongo

Philippine payment gateway — GCash, Maya, credit & debit cards.

📧

Resend (Email)

Transactional emails: booking confirmations, reminders & cancellations.

🧠

OpenAI / Ollama

AI layer powering the staff assistant and the public chatbot widget.

🔐

Supabase Auth

Secure email/password login with role-based access control (RBAC).

☁️

Next.js 15 + Vercel

Server-rendered React app. Fast, SEO-ready, deployed to Vercel edge.
